# E-Commerce Customer Behavior & Revenue Analysis

## Problem Statement
Understanding customer purchasing behavior and identifying key revenue drivers is critical for business growth.

## Dataset
- Online Retail Dataset (~500K transactions)

## Objectives
- Analyze revenue trends
- Identify top products and countries
- Segment customers using RFM analysis

## Key Analysis
- Monthly revenue trend
- Top selling products
- Country-wise revenue
- Customer segmentation

## Key Insights
- Top customers contribute majority of revenue
- High churn risk among low-frequency buyers
- Revenue heavily dependent on few regions

## Business Recommendations
- Focus on retaining high-value customers
- Reduce reliance on discount-heavy products
- Improve engagement for low-frequency users

## Tech Stack
- Python (pandas, numpy, matplotlib, seaborn)
